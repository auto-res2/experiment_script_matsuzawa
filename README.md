## Research Title
Maximizing Transformer Efficiency: Advanced Fine-Tuning Techniques

## Abstract
\begin{abstract}This paper investigates the optimization of large language models (LLMs) through fine-tuning to enhance their performance in domain-specific tasks, which is crucial given the increasing reliance on LLMs in varied applications. The fine-tuning process presents significant challenges due to dataset variability and the inherent complexity of LLMs, necessitating advanced optimization techniques that can adapt effectively under these conditions. We introduce a baseline method, an additional method, and a novel combined method, each employing diverse optimization strategies aimed at improving convergence and overall model performance. Our contributions are validated through extensive experiments, providing detailed performance metrics and comparative analyses with existing optimization methods, thereby demonstrating the superior effectiveness of our proposed optimizers for fine-tuning LLMs.\end{abstract}
